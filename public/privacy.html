Information sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may disclose information to vendors that help us operate the website or fulfill requests, when required by law, or when necessary to protect our rights, customers, or services. The mobile-information restrictions above continue to apply.

Data retention and security

We retain information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described above, legal obligations, and legitimate business records. We use reasonable safeguards, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your choices
